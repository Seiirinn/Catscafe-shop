ABOUT US

Cafe was built in 2003 by a couple who met during the early 90s.
The owners personally run the business and made it a pet-friendly cafe that serves
premium drinks and quality foods for everyone to enjoy along with their fur babies.
They are also the one who prepares and chose the ingredients for their menus.
According to the owners
"The secret ingredient in making each food premium and quality is not just because of
its ingredients but also because of making them with love."
